    Carols from an Irish Cabin
    Experience the warmth and charm of an Irish country Christmas through musical delights, theater and narrative readings. A celebration of holiday traditions that reveal the spirit of the season with family, hearth, and friends.

    Far from Home * Coming to America
    The people who came to this country during the great migration brought their music and ancient tales with them, and that heritage still lives in the songs we love today. RoseWynde will perform music from Celtic and Early American traditions, and highlight its importance to both people long ago and in our time through readings from poets, historians, letters, and stories of the men and women who made the hopeful journey to America.
