Distant Ayres
 Kathryn Cater, harp and Sandy Duffy Norman, flute present an elegant offering of artistry with the bold, emotional, and creative interpretations that brings only pleasure from beginning to end in their second CD, Distant Ayres. These deeply touching arrangements of familiar Celtic and Folk tunes transport the listener back to the days of childhood gardens and mates and the nostalgic feel of summertime picnics with the family. Distant Ayres can not be defined by only one genre, but combines fresh traditional arrangements and the classical sounds of Claude Debussy, Morten Lauridsen, and John Rutter, as well as original works by the duo.
