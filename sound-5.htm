A Merry Little Christmas
 Traditional carols and modern classics of the Christmas season are arranged for flute and harp with original simplicity and beauty. The arrangements are straightforward with contemporary touches. This CD offers festive arrangement with typical RoseWynde beauty and lyricism. This is a winning, unique album that is gentle and reflective. A Merry Little Christmas creates a mood and gives the listener a feeling of a winter well wishing.
