Announcing the release of their first CD

Whisper in the Moonlight
transcriptions for flute and piano

Softly through the night my songs implore you. Come down into the still grove, beloved: Slender treetops rustle and whisper in the moon light.
This translation from the Schubert Lieder sets a tone of tender reflection, countered with intense ardour.


Debra Huddleston studied at the Mozarteum in Salzburg, Austrian and completed her Master of Music-Piano Accompaniment degree at the Hartt School of Music in Hartford, Conneticut. She is adjuct faculty for Linfield College and George Fox University, is accompanist for the the Willamette Master Chorus and Willamette Girlchoir, and is director of music at First Presbyterian Church in Woodburn, Oregon. Debra plays a 7'4" Bosendorfer Model 225 Concert Grand piano.

Sandy Duffy Norman is a flutist and teacher in Salem, Oregon and a graduate of Louisiana State University. After earning a B.S.Mus. and B.M.E., she continued postgraduate work in education at Western Oregon University, was a band director for 15 years, and has maintained Sunridge Flute Studios in Salem since 1984. She released four CDs with RoseWynde, flute and harp duo, since 2000 and published four collections of their arrangements with ALRY Publications. Sandy has performed at three National Flute Conventions, is a member of B.M.I.,on the Greater Portland Flute Society board of directors, and belongs to the MENC/OMEA, and NFA. Sandy plays on a handmade 14K gold Powell flute.
