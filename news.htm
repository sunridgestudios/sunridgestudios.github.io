Recordings by RoseWynde


A Merry Little Christmas
 Traditional carols and modern classics of the Christmas season
are arranged for fluteand harp with original simplicity and beauty.
The arrangements are straightforward with contemporary touches.
This CD offers festive arrangement with typical RoseWynde beauty and lyricism.
This is a winning, unique album that is gentle and reflective.
A Merry Little Christmas creates a mood and gives the listener a feeling of a winter well wishing.




Morning By Morning:
Songs and Hymns for Inspiration
Meditative and joyful music using flute, violin, cello, and harp.
RoseWynde has arranged their favorite hymns and various songs that will uplift the spirit and lighten the soul. Most of the selections are found in your hymnal: Great Is Thy Faithfulness, Nearer My God to Thee, Rock of Ages, Day By Day, and many more. RoseWynde has also included the prayerful Bach Arioso, Meditation, an original compositon by Kathryn Cater, and the traditional Irish Hymn Avondale and Morning has Broken.

Winter Ayres
 Featuring Celtic Christmas Tunes, Baroque selections,
and the best music of the Winter Season.
This CD creates a mood for the winter journey. From harvest to the darkening of the days, Through to the New Year and the return of the light. Winter Ayres presents the most beautiful music of the Baroque by Vivaldi, Pergolesi, and Tartini, the heartfelt Christmas tunes from Ireland, Scotland, and England like Wexford Carol, Auld Lang Syne, Here We Come A-Wassailing, and Sussex Carol, including traditional music of the Season with, What Child Is This, Away in the Manger, Jeanette Isabella, and more.
Winter Ayres

as you step from hearth and candle,
Outside to the glitter of an icy dawn.
Walk fast in the snow and slow in the frost.
Feel the grass crunch underfoot,
every fence, every tree as white as white can be.
Across the fields snow drops drooping on their stalks,
Holly and mistletoe etched in silver lace.
There in your own backyard
Your heart stands still, eyes grow bright,
Winter Ayres tells its song with silent light.
God send you a happy New Year




Distant Ayres
Deeply touching arrangements of familiar Celtic and Folk tunes
transport the listener back to the days of childhood gardens and mates to the nostalgic feel of summertime picnics with the family. Distant Ayres can not be defined by only one genre, but combines fresh traditional arrangements and the classical sounds of Claude Debussy, Morten Lauridsen, and John Rutter, as well as original works by the duo.

For times gone by, breathe in such Distant Ayres.
