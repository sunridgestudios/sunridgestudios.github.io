Morning by Morning :
Songs and Hymns for Inspiration
 Meditative and joyful music using flute, violin, cello, and harp. RoseWynde has arranged their favorite hymns and various songs that will uplift the spirit and lighten the soul. Most of the selections are found in your hymnal: Great Is Thy Faithfulness, Nearer My God to Thee, Rock of Ages, Day By Day, and many more. RoseWynde has also included the prayerful Bach Arioso, Meditation, an original compositon by Kathryn Cater, and the traditional Irish Hymn Avondale and Morning has Broken.
