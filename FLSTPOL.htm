PRIVATE INSTRUCTION

 Professional private guidance for the complete flutist, all levels taught, beginning through college.
Audition and solo preparations are a focus.
These are 1/2-hour weekly lessons.

EXPECTATIONS
  • Students must attend all scheduled lessons.
  • Students must practice daily; parents should actively encourage regular practice.
  • Students should come prepared for their lessons, have all materials, and know the lesson routine.
  • Students are expected to participate in solo and ensemble and the yearly studio recital.
  • Parents should budget for music purchases.
  • Parents should budget for professional accompanists.
  • Parents should budget for instrument repair and upgrades.

STUDIO RECITAL

The annual Studio Recital will be held in the chapel at Our Saviour Lutheran Church,Friday, May 20, 2016 from 4:00-6:00. All students are invited to perform.

AUDITIONS, COMPETITIONS, AND SCHOLARSHIPS

Middle school and high school students are expected to participate in the district solo and ensemble contest sponsored by the OMEA in the spring. I also direct students to opportunities for state and regional honor ensembles such as: Portland Youth Philharmonic, Salem Youth Symphony, Allstate Band, All City Band, All Northwest Band. I will assist students in their preparation for any auditions, as well as for Competitions, College Admissions and Scholarship auditions. I am proud of the high level of playing and commitment my students display. Each year the studio produces District and State Solo Champions, Concerto Competition Finalists, All-Northwest, All-State, and All-City band participants and many thousands of dollars in flute performance scholarships. I look forward to a rewarding relationship with all of my flute students and their families.
